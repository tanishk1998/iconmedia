The Challenge: How to create a truly incredible experience within a considerably limited budget?

To show what truly incredible branding looks and feels like, we brainstormed various ideas around high-fidelity imagery and unique ways to bring them on.

The Idea

The spot revolves around the concept of using powerful colours and imagery to reimagine what traditional digital marketing campaigns would look like. We decided to use a primary color scheme of light green along with a dark theme.

    Design Details

    We worked closely with the TechLane technical Team to get every detail of their vision correct. There were so many thoughtful and precise design decisions that went into creating the Website and Logo– we wanted to ensure those were expressed through the narrative we presented online.
