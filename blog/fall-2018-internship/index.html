Fall 2018 Internship

POSTED BY

Greg Gunn

Want to intern with us? We’re looking for graphic designers, video editors, UX/UI designers, and developers to join us in the Fall. No formal education required—just good work and a good attitude.

You’ll work with our team in Santa Monica, on real projects, and earn some real dough while you’re at it.

Think you’d be a good fit? Email intern@blind.com with your name and skill set as the subject line. Be sure to include a link to your portfolio—no attachments please. International applicants are welcome to apply.

Submissions are open through Friday, July 20th.
