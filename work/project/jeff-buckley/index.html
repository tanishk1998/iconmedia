Jeff Buckley “Just Like A Woman”

Sony

An interactive slice of life

It’s not every day you’re given a chance to work on something special—let alone collaborate with a creative soul that left such a beautiful mark on the world.

Prior collaborators, Interlude, met with Icon Media Creative Director, Greg Gunn, to talk about creating an animated video for the late singer. After a brief conversation with Interlude CEO, Yoni Bloch, everyone felt that we had something special on our hands.

Jeff’s performance of this song—originally by Bob Dylan—is sensational. It’s as if he imagined there was an orchestra behind him while he performed. We wanted to make a video that captured that drama and give him that orchestra.

The end result is a beautiful mixture of artistry from many disciplines. The development team, Interlude, built new technology to achieve the desired effect. And with a blessing from Jeff’s mother, supplemental orchestration was recorded to bolster the original song.

Developing the Story

“The idea for this story came from the maze of emotions that we all experience throughout our relationships. Depending on your emotional perspective, the simplest activities can feel very different."

Greg Gunn, Creative Director

 

To capture this idea, we developed a story that follows a couple over the course of one day. As the viewer, you’re able to control how the characters feel at any given moment by changing their relationship to each other with a series of clicks.

Every decision you make along the way affects key moments in the video that ultimately change the underlying orchestration of the song. This emotional interaction creates a beautiful and personal experience for everyone that watches it.
