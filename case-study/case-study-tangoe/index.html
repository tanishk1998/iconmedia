The Bottom Line

MUJ organizes Jaipur’s biggest cultural fest, Oneiros. In simple words means personification of dream. The fest living upto it’s actual meaning, conducts such events wherein people get together and showcase their talents in various fields like music, dance, acting, writing, fashion, photography, and much more.

Strategy

The project was initiated after a brief conversation with The Director of MUJ. After explaining the theme and giving us an outline of how they wanted Oneiros to represented in the digital, we were underway creating the entire website from scratch.

Several meetings were held in between to get a clearer understanding of the product they were expecting. After overcoming the initial tech jargon, we were able to surface key insights into the how to make the website reach the masses.

 

Design

The IT Director is the lifeblood of all modern companies. Often misunderstood and maligned. We only notice him when things stop working. Imagine your email system crashing down. Who is going to reconnect you to your customers? The IT Director is not just a hero, he is a superhero. And to quote Tangoe he has, “Super Fiscal Vision.”

This notion quickly formed into the design, visuals and messaging for the Tangoe brand and was applied across all key touch points including a video which was led by Icon Media CD, Greg Gunn.
